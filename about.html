About Me

I'm Khadija Rukhsar, a psychology student with a strong interest in understanding people, ideas, and the world around me. My interests include psychology, philosophy, literature, and personal development.

I enjoy exploring questions about human behavior, identity, meaning, and growth. Reading and learning are important parts of my life, and I am constantly seeking new perspectives and knowledge.

This website serves as a space to share thoughts, reflections, and insights gathered through study, reading, and personal experience. It is a collection of ideas, questions, and discoveries from an ongoing journey of learning and self-understanding.

When I'm not studying, you'll usually find me reading a book, reflecting on a new idea, or trying to better understand the complexities of human nature.
