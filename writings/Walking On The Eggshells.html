Walking On The Eggshells

Walking on the road of life, I often stumble and fall. I hear incomprehensible voices and try to see if someone is there talking to me. I encounter some thick smoky shadows luring me into the depths. I see ghosts trying to possess me, but I don’t recognize them. However, they appeal to me on every stance. Ghosts of memories, people and my lost selves. They are stopping me from moving forward. My racing heart and on-edge sensations won’t let me rest in peace. I want to grow but the roots have become weakened with agonies. My feet feel drenched in the ground and the only way I can keep going is by crawling on my knees. My knees hurt too and have many bruises. The shades of blue and purple, the shades of death. I look back and it’s foggy, blocking my view and filling my lungs. I look forward and see a hollow ground. But this ground is separated by a transparent wall. I can touch it like a smart phone and scroll left and right to change what’s ahead of me. Sometimes it’s bloomy and sometimes it’s blank. I see shades of rainbow becoming white light and then vanishing in strong thunder strikes. My hands are tired now and I’m still on my knees. I want to rest my head against this wall and sleep. And when I sleep I demand from everyone, please don’t come across this wall otherwise I might puke. Your voice may reach me but I won’t be able to see you, and your footprints, they would haunt me. So please for my peace, let me sleep.
