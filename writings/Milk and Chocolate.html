Milk and Chocolate

Milk and Chocolate (And Other Shared Sweetness) Cold, foggy days, when the will to get out of bed diminishes. The body seems buried with years of tiredness. From whispers like I wanna die to be present for your loved ones. Life seems not still at times, but moving with tenderness. When efforts made for your loved ones are returned by the universe. And you receive the warmest smiles from strangers. You feel alive. You feel like the parts of you are resurfacing and flourishing. And spreading their fragrance as your aura and your vibes. The parts you never knew existed in you. Buried deep inside. Under the darkness of life. The parts that seemed belonged to others. You witnessed them and thought sadly, it can never be you. You might feel unstoppable. That's the worth of doing something for your loved ones. But it's deeper than that. Making efforts for your loved ones is valuing your love for them. It's another type of self love. Another dimension. That I never knew could exist in myself. I never thought I'd own it. As it seems superficial. With thoughts like, people start doing things for others when they can't do something for themselves. Like it proved their inability to do something for others. While keeping themselves as a pedestal. But you realize now. That it's tangible. You start with others. Others that become the significance of your essence. And you meet parts of you, you never owned. It's not an easy journey. It keeps you exhaling smog. The darkness tries to sing you a lullaby. Making you sleep, tempting you to betray. But its melody is disrupted by a shining star, you can call love. Love that gives life. Even if it itself feels not worthy of existence. Love that gives energy. Love that keeps you hydrated. From the thirst of meaninglessness. Unhappiness. Sadness. Grief. Auras merge when people meet. To find their home or maybe uniqueness. To find if this is the place where they are supposed to be. Or to find a home, so complete, so tender. With mirrors of giggles, intimacy and humor. With fragrances of hunger, mother's love and a friend's shoulder. With a promise that was kept. With a commitment, that'll be met. Sharing worlds and words. Questioning. Discussing. Everything is love. Carrying on the mundane side by side. Wishing it wouldn't contaminate the home's purity. Walks and talks. With swollen feet, frozen hands and aching legs. Witnessing chaos. And feeding a cat. Being vulnerable to hear “it's fine.” Like having the perfect tea at the perfect hour. Taking sides and sticking to opposites. Exploring contrasts and existing anyway. Looking at life being sold on the fancy stalls. Just appreciating the timing to be there feeling home. Some familiar faces with teasing smirks. Some pure souls with overflowing energy. Shifting paths. Matching foot with foot. Digging deeper and deeper. While being shy and embarrassed. Where love laughed and echoed like the only sound. Where every stop became a holy sanctuary. With silver adjusting itself into tangled hearts. Fitting, not fitting, fitting again. Appreciated from time to time. Peeked to be sure that they're there. Going in circles. Having rounds. Finding solace to stare. Tasting sweetness from the surroundings and finding it in your mouth. Then in the gestures of others. And unexpected waves. Carrying emotions like beads. Coming together to conceive. An understanding worth struggling for. Exploring souls. The songs and mournings coming and passing by. Just to find another smile on your face. And then to witness the giggles of a stranger. Sharing sweetness with amplified joy. Laughing exceptionally at the ordinary. An angry cat. That cannot resist its instincts to play. Even if the anger wants her to be limited. Hugs and affection. Exactly what a kid needs. Trust and encouragement. Exactly what a human demands. Words that become confessions. The abstract that becomes life. Hours, minutes, days. In words. And soft whispers. Tomorrow, it may seem like a dream. But it's a dream of courage. Overlapping betrayal and truth. True essence and constraints. Love and Friendship. Milk and chocolate.
