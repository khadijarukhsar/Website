I've to think about a title for this

Oh God! I've to think about a title for this. (Smart, ain't it? ) Or maybe lazy. Oh please! Don't be judgy. I can't read. Even when I try so hard. I miss those days when books could give me an escape. They've lost their value now. I look at them sadly. Pick one to read. Leave it on my bed. Unopened. Then I put it back. And choose another one. And do the same. Our solaces shouldn't abandon us. It's unkind. I can't lift a brush now. Nor a pen. Or a book. Is this a punishment? But for what? Even sleep don't give me that solace now. Things shouldn't stop working all at once. They should consider their prey. Starving from every resource. It's tragic. Those were my blessings. And maybe still are. But there's a very thin line. Between a blessing and a test. If there is a line. Maybe this is a blessing too. A different one. Or maybe this is another test. Who knows what. Life is moody. It keeps changing everything. With the same patterns. And I'm still not used to it. I think this world is not for me. I should shift to the Mars. Maybe I'm living someone else's life. My place should've been somewhere else. No, it's not complaining. I hate doing that. I just felt like this. Like a zombie trying to fit it. I should've been Frankenstein. No, I can't. That's pity. Maybe I'm his wife. But she was never born. Am I born? Oh God, I love it. When I feel high. Without using any substance. It feels great. But I should sleep. I have a tough day ahead. No more feeling high. Time to be unconscious. I hope. I'll. I forgot my bottle of water in the kitchen. I should grab it. And I hate water when it comes into my dreams. It always sacres me. Trying to consume me. I find it hard to breath. And my heart races so fast. Oh, I had to read 2 pages. Before going to bed. But I didn't. I'll try again tomorrow. But I know I won't read tomorrow too. But shush. I'll try. Anyway. Whatever. Author's Note: > At this point, I've certainly stopped processing that others can read it to. But. . . Whatever. Someone please take my phone away. And my life too. Nope. lol.
