Entry 11
Peeking Into the Raw (And hiding from it.) I have a love story A confession to make I won't say it out aloud It's supposed to be a whisper Its plot is my imagination Of all the places I can ever explore But I can feel it residing in my body too It's always present Always alive Becoming my love itself Making it hard for me choose Between my love and this story It lives in the darkness Maybe it is the darkness It has depth of an abyss Maybe it is the abyss It has emotional intensity of a sensitive mind Maybe it's me If you look at it, it stares back Maybe it's a living being However this stare feels hollow Maybe it's a mimicry of existence It feels you Understands you Portrays you Maybe it's a friend But it also suffocates you Maybe it's the fog It steals you, drains you, gives you life Maybe it's sadistic Or sarcastic knives That makes you bleed but keeps you confused If you're using it the right way Or maybe you're the one being used It plays with you, lives with you, make you feel alive Maybe it itself is the life It hugs you, consoles you to let you know you wanna cry A friend to grief or simply a pry It clenches your throat till you gasp for air Maybe it's a beast Playing rough and unfair It has no physical form, it's boundless and limitless However your senses are occupied by it. Maybe it's empirical. A phenomenon or a mess. Have you seen something phenomenological that doesn't make sense at all? Both sound opposing, so maybe it's a paradox Sorry, I lied I've seen it actually But it sees me with its existence factually It exists everywhere And I swear It's not god It can never be It's coward, it hides And still sting like a bee It's malicious It's dark But I've emotions for it And a spark It manipulates me Tell me things So I listen and stay In return it clings It's not a human It can never be It's devoid of empathy An enemy of glee We hold hands It's soothing sometimes But it consumes me Like nothing do It's selfish It's ironic Addictive Seductive It feels like a silky hair Stuck inside your lips There's something romantic about it But you know it's not there It feels like a caregiver Holding a child Making her feel secure But keeping her hungry in the wild The child wails with hunger But the mother is happy As she has a reward to show As something she kept alive It mimics life So how can it give that to others? Shallow breaths Dry lips With ache as care? The child wants its oxygen tank With a muffled cry Wanting attention Like doing a prank I'm running to catch it I don't know where is she I'm guilty because i know It's never there Where it's supposed to be.
