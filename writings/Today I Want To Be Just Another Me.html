Entry 17
Oh concealer, please conceal these spots, I want to make my skin even-toned. Let me forget some people and some crazy plots, Some laughter cried, and some misery moaned. Today, I’m going to hide what I seek pride in. All sorrows today will be thrown in the bin. I want to dance today to celebrate every tragedy. Give me some tequila, please bring me some gin. Keep distant this sadness, today’s not its day. I want to swim in cold today or sit on the bay. Hush and keep these feelings in that hidden box. Look, I am today the sail and the cox. They can’t drive me, or make me sick. Let’s get rid of this constant ick. Today is different, please let it be. Today I want to be just another me.
